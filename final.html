Project Description

My final project was a game made with python and pygame. In the game, you controlled a small paddle with the left and right arrow keys. The point of the game was to avoid the blocks falling from above you. There are multiple levels and in each level, there are more blocks falling at a time. To score in this game, you have to avoid a block and then you will get a point when it passes the screen. As the levels progress, you have to avoid more blocks to go onto the next one.
